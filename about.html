History of Asian College

Founded in 1972, Asian College (formerly known as Asian Institute of Electronics) has been a pioneer in technology-oriented education in the Philippines. With decades of excellence in information technology, business, and communication courses, Asian College has continually adapted to the evolving educational landscape. Its legacy of academic integrity and innovation remains at the heart of its commitment to empowering future professionals.
